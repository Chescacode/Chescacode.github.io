BTS

Introduction

While I do like drawing and making art, I like music, too. One of the bands I listen to is BTS. BTS is a seven-member Korean boyband that debuted in 2013. Unlike the boybands in America, they incorporate a lot of dance into their music videos. Their music is mostly based on the struggles of life, growth as a person, and society's expectations; they also have the cliche love album, too.
